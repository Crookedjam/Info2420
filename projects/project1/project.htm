Web Site Critiques

Trader Joe's  https://www.traderjoes.com

  1. My first impression of the website was that it was full of pictures and information. I liked the hamburger menu it was organized and had some little pictures.

  2. Trader Joe's website has an organized flow of information. It has a hamburger menu that takes you to spesific shoping items.

  3. Trader Joe's site makes sense to me it has shoping and info about upcoming events.

  4. In my opinion I think the home page with all the info like crossword puzzles, fondue, and recipes is a little much for me. If I just want to buy some cheese I might get desracted and forget to buy my cheese.

  5. The load time was pretty fast for me considuring how many pictures there are.

  6. The font color is mostly black against white so its easy to read. It uses headers good as well I always know what the main point is.

  7. There are links on the main page example a "discover more" button to give more info on a specific subject.

  8. The website has a theme all the buttons have shading and the same look.

  9. Trader Joe's site is responsive. I looked at the site in half screen just fine.

  10. I liked that it was very organized and at the bottomm of each page they had contact info. Very well done.
